Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026  ·  Effective from: 25 June 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Quick summary: We collect your income tax data only to file your ITR. We never sell your data. Documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. Your PAN, Aadhaar, and financial data are handled under strict security controls in compliance with the IT Act 2000, DPDP Act 2023, and ICAI guidelines.

1. About us

TaxTalk is a conversational income tax return (ITR) filing platform operated by RB Finsol Private Limited (formerly trading as R B Shah & Associates), a Chartered Accountancy firm with over 30 years of practice, registered under the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). Our registered office is in Rajkot, Gujarat, India.

This Privacy Policy applies to the TaxTalk web and mobile application, accessible at taxtalk.rbfinsol.in and related subdomains ("Platform"), and all services provided through it.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Identity & KYC information

  • Full name, date of birth, gender
  • Permanent Account Number (PAN)
  • Aadhaar number (last 4 digits only stored — full number used only for e-verification and never persisted)
  • Mobile number and email address
  • Residential address (locality, city, state, PIN code)

2.2 Financial & tax information

  • Salary, business income, freelance receipts, capital gains, interest income, rent income
  • TDS deducted (sourced from Form 16, AIS, Form 26AS)
  • Deductions claimed under Chapter VI-A (80C, 80D, 80E, 80G, etc.)
  • Bank account number and IFSC code (for ITR refund and e-filing)
  • Challan details for advance tax and self-assessment tax
  • Balance sheet and P&L figures (for business/professional clients)

2.3 Documents you upload

Form 16, AIS, Form 26AS, broker capital gain statements, balance sheets, P&L accounts, and other income tax documents you voluntarily upload for AI-assisted data extraction.

2.4 Automatically collected information

  • IP address (used for rate limiting and security logging only)
  • Device type, browser, operating system
  • Session timestamps and page interactions (for audit trail and debugging)
  • Cookies — see Section 9 below

3. How we use your information

  • To prepare and file your Income Tax Return for the relevant Assessment Year
  • To enable your Chartered Accountant at RB Shah & Associates to review and approve your return
  • To extract structured data from your tax documents using AI (Anthropic Claude API) — documents are processed in real-time and not used to train any AI model
  • To communicate with you about your return status, queries from the CA, and refund/payment notifications
  • To comply with ICAI record retention requirements (7 years post-filing)
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access
  • To improve the Platform's accuracy and user experience (using aggregate, anonymised data only)

We do not use your financial data for advertising, credit scoring, insurance, or any purpose outside ITR filing services.

4. Legal basis for processing

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and the Information Technology Act, 2000, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent — you provide explicit consent at account creation (recorded with timestamp, version, and IP)
  • Contractual necessity — processing is required to deliver the ITR filing service you have engaged us for
  • Legal obligation — we are required to maintain records under ICAI, Income Tax Act, and IT Act provisions
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention and platform security

5. Data sharing

We share your data only as follows:

  • Income Tax Department of India — your ITR JSON/XML is transmitted directly to the e-filing portal (incometax.gov.in) through our CA's ERI credentials or downloaded by you
  • Supabase Inc. — our database and authentication provider; data stored in their Singapore region data centre under SOC 2 Type II certification
  • Cloudflare Inc. — encrypted document storage (R2) with zero-knowledge object storage; Cloudflare has no ability to read document contents
  • Anthropic PBC — AI extraction API; documents are sent over TLS 1.3; Anthropic's API does not retain or train on submitted data per their enterprise data processing agreement
  • CA staff at RB Shah & Associates — your CA and assigned staff review your return data for professional compliance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, banks, insurance companies, or any third party for commercial purposes.

6. Data security

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3
  • Database columns containing sensitive data (PAN, bank details) are accessible only via Row Level Security (RLS) — each user can only access their own data
  • Full Aadhaar numbers are never stored — only the last 4 digits are retained post-KYC
  • Identity fields (PAN, name, DOB) are locked once verified and cannot be changed without CA authorisation, enforced at the database trigger level
  • API keys (AI service, storage service) are stored only as server-side environment variables — never in source code or exposed to client browsers
  • All document URLs are time-limited presigned URLs (valid 5 minutes) — no permanent public links exist
  • An immutable audit log records every significant action (uploads, approvals, deletions, CA reviews)
  • Access to the CA dashboard is role-restricted with database-level enforcement
  • Regular dependency audits and security reviews are conducted before each release

7. Data retention

  • Tax returns and documents: retained for 7 years post-filing (ICAI requirement is 6 years; we retain 7 for safety buffer)
  • Chat conversation logs: retained for 3 years
  • Audit logs: retained for 7 years (immutable, no delete policy)
  • Consent records: retained for the duration of the account plus 3 years
  • On account deletion: personal profile data is deleted within 30 days; tax return records are retained for the statutory period and then permanently deleted

8. Your rights under DPDP Act 2023

  • Right to access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate data (identity fields require CA verification)
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of data not required for statutory compliance
  • Right to data portability — download your ITR JSON and extracted data at any time from the Platform
  • Right to withdraw consent — you may withdraw consent, but this will prevent us from providing ITR services; statutory retention obligations still apply
  • Right to nominate — nominate a person to exercise rights on your behalf

To exercise these rights, contact our Grievance Officer (see Section 11).

9. Cookies

TaxTalk uses the following cookies:

  • sb-auth-token (Essential) — Supabase authentication session token. Stores your login session. Without this cookie, you cannot log in. Duration: session + 1 hour auto-refresh. No personal financial data is stored in this cookie.
  • taxtalk_lang (Functional) — stores your selected language preference (English/Hindi/Gujarati). Duration: persistent (localStorage). Can be cleared by clearing browser storage.
  • taxtalk_pwa_dismissed (Functional) — remembers if you dismissed the "Add to Home Screen" prompt. Duration: session.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. No data is shared with advertising networks.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this will prevent login functionality.

10. Children's privacy

TaxTalk is not intended for persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.
